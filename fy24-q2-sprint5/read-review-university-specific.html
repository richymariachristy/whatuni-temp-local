Law LLB (Hons)

University rating (4)

There are campuses across the country and you can switch between them whenever you'd like in order to find one that suits you. The teaching style is refreshingly modern, and the staff have all been friendly and helpful. The university is very accommodating of disabled students such as myself and were proactive when making arrangements for me. The number of societies and activities on the smaller campuses is fairly minimal, though. Something to keep in mind if you're wanting to get involved!!

The course is taught through blended learning - I like this, but I appreciate that is isn't for everyone.

Postgraduate Diploma in Law (PGDL)

University rating (4)

As an international student, starting a new educational journey can be daunting. But, the University of Law made it enjoyable and seamless. While the facilities and team are noteworthy, it's the personal tutors and professors who make the university stand out. ULaw's relationships between students and faculty are exceptional. The support and guidance I received from my tutors and professors surpassed all expectations. Compared to my friends at other universities, ULaw's tutors are the real deal. They offer job opportunities, law and business fairs, seminars, and have an amazing employability team. Basically, ULaw is dedicated to providing an inclusive and supportive environment, with amazing faculty. Pursuing your academic and professional aspirations at ULaw is an assured choice.

Facilities: 4.0
Location: 4.0
Student support: 5.0
Campus look and feel: 4.0
Student life: 4.0
Students' union: 4.0
Course rating (4)

What I appreciate about PGDL is the exceptional structure of the course. The study materials are also impressive. However, I would advise new students that it's possible to struggle with understanding the concepts in the initial stages of the course. In that case, I recommend booking an appointment with your personal tutor to avoid affecting your preparation. Overall, I'm really enjoying my experience here and would highly recommend this university to anyone looking to further their legal education.
